Use Zidovudine is used in HIV infected patients only in combination with at least 2 other ARV drugs. However, its efficacy as monotherapy in AIDS has been confirmed in the past. HIV-RNA titer is reduced to undetectable levels and CD4 count increases progressively. Immune status is improved and opportunistic infections become less common. There is a sense of well-being and patients gain weight. AZT also reduces neurological manifestations of AIDS and new Kaposi's lesions do not appear. Mortality among AIDS patients is reduced. It has also been shown to slow the progression of HIV infection, including escalation of ARC to full blown AIDS. However, beneficial effects are limited from a few months to a couple of years after which progressively nonresponsiveness develops.
